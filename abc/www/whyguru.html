Why Guruprasad Concept

Business Solution : Mainly Two Motive is solved by this Concept

1) Business Retention : Through regular return business via register customer , business has more possibility of retention

1) Business Growth : Increase in the customer base on the regular basis through company promotion and publicity . Therefore business grow with increase in repeated customer and new customer.

There are three ways that we can run this business

a) Shops Own and Run By Guruprasad : Shops will be own By Guruprasad Company. Complete Business Operation Manage   by Guruprasad . There will be Legal agreement  between Investors and Guruprasad Company for Certain period which will be followed by both parties .

b) Shops will be own by Investors : Shops will be own by investors , Guruprasad company will manage the shop operation , software and will share business profit with investors . There will be Legal contract between Investors and Guruprasad Compan y for Certain period which will be followed by both parties .

c) Gurprasad Will Manage Loyalty program : Guruprasad will be Tie-up with Shops for running loyalty program on commission basis and Marketing fees . Company will get income through regular monthly commission from Tie-up shops .

Guruprasad Will Manage Loyalty program

*Initially Guruprasad Running its current Business In this scenario .

*Company through various ways by online and offline register and engage its customer base for loyalty program.

*Simultaneously company register shops for its loyalty program

*Guruprasad then promote this shops to the customer for their regular purchase.

*Through various media Guruprasad company provide information to customer of all the products and services attached with Company on regular Basis.

*Since company has Loyalty benefit program for who ever purchase through company , we encourage customer to buy through our channel or through shops attached with company .

*This way, ones a customer attached to the company is there engage for long term with channel and shops attached to company.
