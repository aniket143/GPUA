What is a Guruprasad CRP (Customer Retention Programme )?

Guruprasad CRP is the loyalty base purchasing system offered by Guruprasad Multi-trade marketing private limited (GPMMPL).IN which company provides Loyalty benefits to regular and loyal register customer.

what is mean by Loyalty reward points?

When purchase through the shops tie-up with Guruprasad company , shopkeeper will register every purchase of the customer through guruprasad's Shop APP . on every purchase company allot some calculated points according to the amount and category of the purchase . this points are called Loyalty Reward Points .

How do I earn a Loyalty Reward points ?

Register customer complete a purchase above 500 rupees , customer is eligible for loyalty reward points .When above 100 reward is collected in your a/c then you are qualified to redeem this points ,this loyalty reward points is remitted to your GPMMPL account by company . Loyalty reward points are derived by the company on the basis of overall business done by the company and your regular consistent purchase .Only those customer can avail this loyalty Reward Points who are register with GPMMPL .

How much loyalty reward Points I have earn ?

Every register customer is given a GPMMPL Customer account . Customer can login into GPMMPL a/c can see his all details in my Income details link how much Loyalty Reward Points they have earn .

What is GPMMPL Customer a/c ?

Every customer register with GPMMPL is given a special Customer account with customer idand password for eg. A00501 . where he /she can see their all transaction , every purchase details , done at any GPMMPL related shop . Here Customer can see , add their profile details , purchase details , loyalty Reward Points details , and all other facility available to Customer.

How do I Pay?

Customer can pay for their purchase through cash on shop cash counter . For Online purchase Cash on delivery or Online Payment. Alternatively, one can pay at the time of delivery by cash on Delivery Option. At the time of checkout customer should mention the preferred mode of payment (online payment, cash).

Can I redeem Loyalty Reward Points ?

Yes , you can .When you go to our tie-up shop or services for new purchase your available loyalty reward points amount is shown in shop app while you make purchase . You can choose to redeem those points while purchase as payment amount . that much amount will be reduce from from your Bill amount .

Delivery Charge?

There is no delivery charge Above 500 Rupees for only online purchase.
